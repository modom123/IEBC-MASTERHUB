Security
Bank-Level Security
Your financial data is protected at every layer — from the moment you sign in to every transaction synced from your bank.
🔐
256-bit SSL/TLS
All data in transit is encrypted with the same standard used by major banks and the U.S. government. Your connection is always secure.
📱
Two-Factor Auth
Login requires both your password and a one-time code sent to your phone or authenticator app. No one gets in without your device.
🛡
PCI DSS Compliant
Payment and card data meets Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards. Your card details are tokenized — never stored in plain text.
🔒
Encryption at Rest
All stored financial records, payroll data, and documents are encrypted at rest using AES-256. Even database admins cannot read your data.
📋
Full Audit Log
Every login, change, and transaction is timestamped and logged. You can always see who did what, when — and so can your accountant.
⏱
Auto Session Timeout
Inactive sessions are automatically logged out after a set period. Your account can't be left open on a shared or unattended computer.
💾
Automated Backups
Your data is backed up daily to geographically redundant servers. In the event of any outage, your financial records are always recoverable.
🏦
Read-Only Bank Access
Bank linking uses read-only credentials. We can see and import your transactions — but we can never move, transfer, or touch your money.
